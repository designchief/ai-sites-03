Features

Cover

  • Private Cars and Commercial Vehicles - Comprehensive
  • Reduced to Third Party Fire and Theft for named drivers under 23 and/or provisional licence holders
  • Special Types - third Party Only

Driving

  • Open driving aged 23 to 69 inclusive. Full open driving for Special Types only.

Excess

  • €315 Accidental Damage Fire and Theft increasing to €625 AD for drivers aged 23 to 25 inclusive.

Vehicles

  • Any vehicle owned by, hired loaned or leased to the proposer but excluding any vehicle owned by an employee or director.
  • Subject to annual declaration
  • Mini fleet available (2,3,4 vehicles) subject to immediate declaration of all vehicles

Key Standard Additional Benefits

  • Breakdown Recovery for Private Cars and LVCs up to 2 tonne GVW. (Only available for vehicles registered in ROI)
  • Unlimited windscreen cover
  • Policy cover for Private Cars in Europe
  • Policy cover is available for CV in Europe if agreed in advance
  • Contingency Cover for emplayees or hired in drivers

Key Optional Additional Benefits

(available on request and at the discretion of Allianz)

  • Cover available for vehicles owned by Directors or their spouses. Individual certificates issued
  • Fleet risk management including driver training
  • Comprehensive cover for named drivers under 23 and provisional licence holders
  • Occasional Business Use

Claims

When your client makes a claim, they need an insurance provider that understands their loss and will be there at the crucial moments, providing them with the necessary help to get them trading again as quickly as possible.

We'll provide your client with:

  • A dedicated claims handler
  • Out of hours helpline – FNOL

If you wish to report a new claim, discuss an existing claim, avail of our claims emergency services or breakdown assistance you can call us on 01 613 3990. If you are calling outside of the Republic of Ireland please contact us on 00 353 1 613 3990.
