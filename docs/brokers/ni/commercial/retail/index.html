Retail Insurance

Our online Retail product is suitable for most types of independently owned retail businesses. From restaurants to newsagents, butchers to grocers, it offers a wide range of cover to provide an insurance solution for your client’s business.

Features

  • Trade Contents – up to £750,000 per location
  • Money – following theft or attempted theft of money
  • Goods in Transit – up to £10,000
  • Deterioration of Stock – up to £5,000 as standard
  • Business Interruption – standard limit of £750,000, with a maximum optional limit of up to £2,000,000, with an indemnity period of 24 months if unable to trade
  • Employers’ Liability – up to £10,000,000
  • Public and Products Liability – up to £2,000,000 with the option to increase to £5,000,000
  • Commercial Legal Expenses – provides up to £100,000 for any one event and up to £1,000,000 for any one period of insurance for selected events
  • Average free – download our guidance notes for sums insured to ensure that your customers are fully protected.

  • Data carrying media (referred to as computer systems records under Contents) not exceeding €10,000
  • Temporary Removal of Deeds and Documents not exceeding €10,000
  • Temporary Removal of Computer System Records not exceeding €2,500
  • Contractor’s Interest
  • Damage to Grounds and Landscaped Gardens by emergency services up to €25,000
  • Locks & Keys up to €10,000
  • Trace and Access for damage by an insured peril up to €25,000
  • Theft Damage to Buildings not exceeding €25,000
  • Metered Utilities not exceeding €25,000
  • Exhibitions not exceeding €5,000
  • Further Investigation Expenses not exceeding €5,000
  • Undamaged Stock not exceeding 15% of the stock sum insured
  • Alternative Residential Accommodation not exceeding 25% of the sum insured of the household goods sum insured
  • Seasonal Increase in Stock – 40% November, December, January & 30 days to Easter Sunday
  • Goods in Transit limit not exceeding €5,000
  • Tenants Liability to Landlord not exceeding €5,000 or 15% of the household goods sum insured whichever is less
  • Breakage or Collapse of Television and Radio Aerials or Masts not exceeding €5,000
  • Capital Additions not exceeding 10% of total sum insured or €500,000 whichever is less
  • European Union & Public Authority Clause
  • Theft damage to buildings
  • Adjoining Buildings
  • Day One Reinstatement
  • Workman’s Clause for contracts not exceeding a value of €50,000
  • Non-Invalidation Clause
  • Contracting Purchasers Interest

Money Cover

  • Custodian Levels €4,000 - €15,000
  • Total Disablement €85 per week
  • Damage to clothing and personal effects €650
  • Vouched medical expenses €500
  • Fraud/Dishonesty money exclusion 14 days
  • Money in gaming/vending machines €500

Business Interruption

  • Professional Accountants Clause
  • Notifiable Disease, Prevention of Access, Public Utilities up to €250,000 any one loss or €250,000 any one period of insurance
  • Public relation expenses up to €10,000

Employer’s Liability

  • Court Attendance
  • Contractual Liability
  • Indemnity to Other Parties includes Indemnity to Principals, Personal Representatives,

Public Liability

  • Indemnity to Other Parties includes Indemnity to Principals, Personal Representatives,
    Other Persons and Domestic Employees
  • Motor Contingency
  • Data Protection up to €250,000 any one loss or €250,000 any one period of insurance
  • Wrongful Arrest will not exceed €20,000 for any one incident or €100,000 in any one Period of Insurance
  • Court Attendance: compensation for attendance at court will not exceed €500 per day in
    respect of any partner or director and €250 per day in respect of any employee.

Legal Expenses

  • No compensation award cover for disputes over collective bargaining of terms and conditions under the Industrial Relations Acts*
  • The compensation awards exclusion in relation to maternity cover now also excludes paternity, adoption or Parental Rights*
  • Debt Recovery - Debts purchased from a third party are not covered
  • Cover for defamation is excluded
  • Judicial reviews – exclusion extended to include coroners inquests, injunctions and fatal accident inquiries

*Cover remains for defence of a legal action and it is the compensation award that is excluded

For full details download our policy document or contact our small business team.

Risk Appetite

Preferred Trades:

  • Specialised food and drinks stores - Restaurants, Café, Deli & Take-aways
  • DIY and hardware stores
  • Fashion, footwear and textiles stores
  • Books, newspapers and stationery stores
  • Furniture, lighting and homewares stores
  • Hair & Beauty – Hairdressers, Barbers, Beauticians, Nail bars.
  • Computers, electrical and electronics stores
  • Supermarkets and convenience stores (grocers, butchers, fishmongers, off-licences, health food, bakeries, etc. )
  • Other non-food specialised stores (music, toys, garden centres, sports, motor factors, etc. )
  • Department stores.

Trades we will not quote for:

  • Tanning salons
  • Estate agents with more than 10% turnover in property management
