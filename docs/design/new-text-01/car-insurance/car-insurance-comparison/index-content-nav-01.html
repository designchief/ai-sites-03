Other Benefits

Make the comparisons below and see the benefits for yourself! The below benefits are available on Allianz comprehensive car insurance cover. Standard excess of €250 applies.

Protected No Claims Bonus

If you add Bonus Protection to your policy you may incur one unlimited third party or accidental damage claim in any consecutive three-year renewal period without this having an impact on your No Claim Bonus entitlement. In addition, any claim payment for windscreen breakage, fire or theft will be paid without impact on your No Claim Bonus entitlement.

Get quote

Claims Notification & Emergency Service

The Emergency assistance service is available to all policyholders 24/7/365 in the event of fire, vandalism, theft or attempted theft. In the event of an accident the assistance service is available if you have Comprehensive cover. As part of the service, we will get you home safely, tow your car to one of our approved garages and give you a free replacement car for up to 5 days while yours is being repaired. If your car is stolen we will give you a replacement car for up to 14 days at no extra cost.

Get quote

Breakdown Assistance

This can be added as an optional extra. The service is available 24/7/365 and it provides assistance if your car breaks down, won't start or has a puncture that you need help to fix. It complements our already established Motor Claims Notification and Emergency Service.

Benefits:

  • We will send a competent repairer to assist you at the breakdown site.
  • We will cover the cost of towing your car up to a maximum of 50 KM or 30 miles to the nearest repairer or to your own garage, whichever is closest.
  • A free courtesy car for up to 48 hours.
